Welcome to Jesus Gospel Power

Jesus Gospel Power is a vibrant, spirit-filled ministry dedicated to spreading the good news of Jesus Christ to all nations. Founded in 2001,By Dr.Bishop Augustine Rugut we have grown from a small Bible study group to a thriving church impacting thousands of lives.

We believe in the authority of Scripture, the power of prayer, and the gifts of the Spirit operating in the church today.


Whether you're new to faith or have been walking with Christ for years, we invite you to join us in worship, fellowship, and service. Together, we can make a difference in our community and beyond.


Vision

The purpose of Jesus Gospel Power Ministries Church is to preach the gospel of Jesus Christ and manifest the power of God through our lives for glory and honor of God


Mission

It is a ministry of reconciliation - we reconcile the world to God. - 2nd Corinthians 5:17-18 we equip people so as to usher them into their divine purpose and destiny - Isaiah 26:2
